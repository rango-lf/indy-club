About Indy Club

Indy Club is a student-run discipline club at the University of Toronto. Each and every Industrial Engineering student at U of T is a member and is encouraged to participate in Indy Club activities and events!

Your Indy Club Executives are an elected team of members with the common goal to enhance your student experience. Your Executives work hard throughout the year to hold amazing events for all Indys, and to represent the Industrial Engineering student body to faculty, UTSU, Skule, and the professional world.

Being an active member of Indy Club is a great way to meet new people—the first and easiest step to learning more about what it means to be an Industrial Engineer at the University of Toronto and beyond.

Get Involved

Consider joining the Indy Club Executives! Indy Club runs many events and works on initiatives and projects behind the scenes — requiring help from many people in different roles.

Stay Connected

Follow us on social media and check your student email for updates on upcoming events, opportunities, and announcements from your Executives.
